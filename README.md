# X-Post-Finder

X Post Finder is a Chrome browser extension designed to help users discover, organize, and analyze content on X (formerly Twitter).

## Core Features

### 1. Automatic Post Discovery

While browsing X, the extension automatically scans posts in your timeline and matches them against your configured keywords.

Examples:

* AI
* Giveaway
* Airdrop
* Crypto
* Gaming
* Open Source
* Custom keywords

Posts that match your criteria are automatically identified and recorded.

### 2. Intelligent Scoring System

The extension evaluates posts using multiple factors, including:

* Presence of target keywords
* Engagement metrics (likes, reposts, and replies)
* Author influence
* Presence of images, videos, or links
* User-defined scoring weights

This helps users quickly identify high-value and relevant content.

### 3. Automatic Recording

All matched posts are stored locally for later review and analysis.

Recorded information may include:

* Post URL
* Publication time
* Author
* Matched keywords
* Score
* Viewed status

This makes it easy to organize and revisit important content.

### 4. Recommendation List

The extension generates a recommendation list based on post scores.

Users can:

* View top-rated posts
* Sort by score
* Sort by date
* Filter by keywords

This allows quick access to the most relevant and valuable content.

### 5. Multilingual Interface

Version 1.1.16 supports:

* Chinese
* English

Users can also choose their preferred default language.

### 6. Fully Local Operation

The extension primarily operates within the browser:

* Local configuration storage
* Local score calculation
* Local post recording

No user data is uploaded to third-party servers.

## Who Is It For?

### Content Creators

Discover trending topics and highly engaging posts.

### Developers

Track technology, open-source projects, and AI-related discussions.

### Cryptocurrency Enthusiasts

Follow airdrops, project updates, and community activities.

### Marketing Professionals

Identify trending content and industry insights.

### General Users

Filter information efficiently and improve the browsing experience on X.


## How to Use:

Install the extension and sign in to X (Twitter).
Open the extension and configure your keywords and preferences.
Browse content on X, and the extension will automatically identify relevant posts.
Review the scoring results and recommended posts.
Manually choose which posts you are interested in and interact with them as desired.
